Explore our services:

  • Dental Bonding

    Dental bonding repairs minor cosmetic issues using a tooth-colored material, similar to composite fillings, that blends seamlessly with your natural teeth.

  • Invisalign

    Invisalign offers an effective way to straighten your teeth without the need for braces. Dr. Charles Dental specializes in Invisalign to help patients achieve a beautiful, aligned smile.

  • Teeth Whitening

    Brighten your smile with our professional teeth whitening services using KöR products to safely and effectively enhance the whiteness of your teeth.

  • Veneers

    Veneers provide a cosmetic solution to minor imperfections like chips, cracks, or discoloration. Contact Dr. Charles Dental to learn how veneers can revitalize your smile.

  • Smile Makeovers

    A complete smile makeover is a custom treatment plan designed to address your unique concerns, from teeth whitening to alignment and much more.

Our Mission

At Dr. Charles Dental, we are committed to providing high-quality dental care, blending both aesthetic and full-mouth rehabilitation services with a focus on overall wellness. Our distinctive approach lies in our thorough evaluation of each case, addressing the root causes of dental concerns to enhance long-term function and beauty. We offer expert referrals, advanced diagnostics, and personalized treatments to ensure lasting results.

Under the leadership of Dr. Charles, a skilled dentist trained in advanced comprehensive care, our dedicated team ensures each patient receives individualized attention and care. Known for his approachable manner and clear communication, Dr. Charles and his associates are trusted by our patients to deliver exceptional results. With a focus on aesthetic and comprehensive dentistry, we aim to leave every patient satisfied and proud to be part of our welcoming community.
